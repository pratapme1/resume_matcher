Job Description: Product Manager 



Role Overview

 The Product Manager identifies customer needs and the larger business objectives that a product or feature will fulfill, articulates what success looks like for a product, and rallies a team to turn that vision into reality. The role is responsible for setting the product roadmap and vision, aligning stakeholders, and ensuring successful end-to-end product rollout. 



 Education:

 Bachelor’s or master’s degree in engineering, Computer Science, Business Administration, or a related field.


 Experience:

 • Total exp - 15 yrs and Relevant 5+ years of experience in product management or related roles within a technology-driven environment.
 • Demonstrated success in managing the end-to-end lifecycle of digital products or platforms.
 • Proven track record of leading cross-functional teams and delivering results in a fast-paced environment.
 • Experience in organizing and managing large-scale technology events (e.g., hackathons, innovation days) is highly desirable.


 Key Responsibilities

 • Product Vision & Strategy

 o Define and communicate the product vision and strategy in alignment with business objectives.
 o Set and maintain the product roadmap, ensuring alignment with organizational goals.
 o Identify customer pain points and translate them into actionable product requirements.
 o Position new products in line with customer demands and market trends. 

 • Stakeholder Management

 o Align stakeholders around the product vision and strategy.
 o Deliver technical information on new and updated products to relevant teams.
 o Foster a shared understanding across larger teams to empower independent decision-making. 

 • Product Delivery

 o Lead the end-to-end rollout of products and features.
 o Ensure timely delivery and high-quality execution of product initiatives.
 o Continuously monitor product performance and iterate based on feedback and data. 

 • Customer Focus

 o Gather and analyze customer feedback to inform product decisions.
 o Ensure products meet or exceed customer expectations and deliver value. 

 Additional Responsibility: Organization-Level Tech Event Management
 • Tech Event Leadership 

 o Plan, organize, and manage organization-level technology events (e.g., hackathons, innovation days, product showcases).
 o Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure successful event execution.
 o Drive engagement and participation across the organization and external partners.
 o Measure and report on event outcomes, leveraging insights to improve future events.
 o Represent the product and organization at internal and external tech events, fostering a culture of innovation and collaboration.


 Skills Required:

 • Proven experience in product management, preferably in a technology-driven environment.
 • Strong leadership, communication, and stakeholder management skills.
 • Demonstrated ability to manage multiple priorities and deliver results.
 • Experience in organizing and managing large-scale tech events is highly desirable.
 • Analytical mindset with a passion for solving customer problems.
